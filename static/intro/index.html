Intro

I'm a master student in theoretical physics, currently enrolled in a double degree between ENSTA ParisTech (Saclay, France) and KTH Royal Institute of Technology (Stockholm, Sweden). I am passionate about research in machine learning, physics and mathematics, trying to entangle those three fields into my research projects. I am currently doing my master's thesis at the University of Toronto with Pr. Peter Wittek, working closely with the start-up Xanadu. My thesis consists in enhancing machine learning algorithms using photonic quantum computers.
